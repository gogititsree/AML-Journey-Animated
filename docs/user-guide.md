# User Guide
## ACAMS CAMS Certification & AML/KYC Interview Prep Tool

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** May 2, 2026

---

## Welcome

This tool is built specifically to help you study for the ACAMS CAMS exam and prepare for AML/KYC compliance job interviews. Everything is in one place — an animated intro video, 200 practice questions, 5 timed mock tests, 200 digital flashcards, and an AML-themed crossword puzzle.

There is nothing to install, no account to create, and no login required. Just open the app in your browser and start studying.

---

## Getting Around

At the very top of the screen you will see five tabs:

| Tab | What it contains |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Animated Story** | A narrated animated video of your career journey |
| **200 Practice Qs** | Individual practice questions you can filter and shuffle |
| **5 Mock Tests** | Full-length timed exam simulations (50 questions each) |
| **200 Flashcards** | Digital flip cards organized by CAMS Study Guide chapter |
| **Crossword** | An interactive puzzle using AML/KYC terminology |

Click any tab to switch to it. Your progress in each section is preserved while you move between tabs — for example, you can be halfway through a mock test, switch to check a flashcard, and come back right where you left off.

## Tab 1 — Animated Story

This is the opening screen. It plays an animated video that tells the story of transitioning from an Enrolled Agent career into AML compliance — covering why financial crime matters, what the CAMS exam covers, and what the job looks like day-to-day.

**Controls:**
- Press the **Play** button to start the animation
- Use the **progress bar** to skip ahead or rewind
- The animation is divided into scenes; each scene introduces a key concept

This tab is a good starting point if you are brand new to the AML world. It gives you the big picture before you dive into the detailed study materials.

## Tab 2 — 200 Practice Questions

This is a bank of 200 multiple-choice questions covering every major topic on the CAMS exam.

### How to Use It

1. **Read the question** at the top of the card
2. **Click one of the four answer choices**
3. The correct answer is immediately highlighted in **green**. If you chose wrong, your selection shows in **red** and the right answer is shown in green
4. Read the **explanation** below the answer choices — this is where the real learning happens
5. Click **Next** to move to the following question

### Topics Covered
The 200 questions span 14 subject areas:
- Bank Secrecy Act (BSA)
- FATF & International Standards
- Money Laundering Typologies
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR)
- Know Your Customer (KYC) / Customer Due Diligence (CDD)
- Politically Exposed Persons (PEP)
- Sanctions & OFAC
- Correspondent Banking
- Trade-Based Money Laundering (TBML)
- Terrorist Financing
- Virtual Assets & Cryptocurrency
- Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
- Transaction Monitoring
- Beneficial Ownership

### Filtering by Topic
Use the **category dropdown** at the top to focus on one subject area at a time. This is useful when you know you are weak in a specific area and want to drill it.

### Shuffling Questions
Click **Shuffle** to randomize the order of questions. This prevents you from memorizing question sequences rather than the actual content.

### Tracking Your Score
Your running score appears at the top — for example "47 / 60 correct." This resets when you use the Reset button or change the category filter.

### Resetting
Click **Reset** to start the entire question bank over from the beginning.

## Tab 3 — 5 Mock Tests

Each mock test simulates exam conditions with 50 questions organized around a specific section of the CAMS Study Guide.

### The Five Tests

| Test | Subject | CAMS Chapters |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Test 1 | Financial Crime Methods & Typologies | Chapters 1–2 |
| Test 2 | Sector-Specific Money Laundering Risks | Chapters 2–4 |
| Test 3 | Global Standards & Regulatory Frameworks | Chapters 5–6 |
| Test 4 | Building an AFC Compliance Program | Chapters 7–8 |
| Test 5 | Technology, Data & Investigations | Chapters 9–10 |

### How to Take a Mock Test

1. From the **test selection screen**, click the test you want to take
2. Answer each of the 50 questions one at a time — same format as the practice questions
3. After all 50 questions, you will see a **Results screen** showing your score, a pass/fail indicator, and a breakdown of which questions you got right and wrong
4. Use the **Back** button on the results screen to return to the test selection menu and choose a different test

### Study Tip
Take the mock tests in order the first time through. This mirrors the chapter progression of the CAMS Study Guide. After you have taken all five, retake the tests where your score was lowest.

### What Counts as Passing?
The actual CAMS exam passing score is approximately 75%. Aim for 75% or higher on each mock test (38+ out of 50) before sitting for the real exam.

## Tab 4 — 200 Flashcards

Flashcards are the fastest way to lock in definitions, acronyms, and key concepts. There are 200 cards spread across all 10 chapters of the CAMS Study Guide — 20 cards per chapter.

### The Ten Chapters

| Chapter | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| Ch. 1 | Understanding Financial Crime |
| Ch. 2 | ML Risks in Financial Services |
| Ch. 3 | ML Risks in Non-Bank Financial Institutions |
| Ch. 4 | ML Risks in DNFBPs & Other Sectors |
| Ch. 5 | Global AFC Frameworks |
| Ch. 6 | AFC Regulations |
| Ch. 7 | Building an AFC Program |
| Ch. 8 | Transaction Monitoring & Investigation |
| Ch. 9 | Technology for AFC Compliance |
| Ch. 10 | Data Collection & Preparation |

### How to Use the Flashcards

1. You will see a card showing a **question or term** on the front
2. **Click the card** to flip it — the answer or definition appears on the back with a smooth 3D animation
3. Click the card again to flip back to the front
4. After reading the answer, choose one of two buttons:
   - **Got It** — you know this card; it counts toward your "mastered" total
   - **Still Learning** — you need more practice with this one
5. The counter at the top shows how many cards you have marked in each pile

### Filtering by Chapter
Use the **chapter selector** at the top to focus on one chapter at a time. Each chapter has its own color accent to help you orient yourself.

### Review Mode
Click **Show: Still Learning** to see only the cards you have not yet mastered. Use this in the days before your exam to focus exclusively on weak spots.

### Shuffling
Click **Shuffle** to randomize the card order within the current chapter or across all chapters.

### Resetting
Click **Reset** to clear all "Got It" and "Still Learning" marks and start fresh.

### Study Tip
Go through all 200 cards in order first to get a baseline. Then switch to "Still Learning" mode and keep cycling through until the pile shrinks to zero.

## Tab 5 — Crossword Puzzle

The crossword uses 30 AML/CAMS terms as answers, each with a clue based on the term's real-world meaning. It is a great change of pace that reinforces terminology in a more engaging way.

### The Words
The puzzle includes terms like: PLACEMENT, LAYERING, INTEGRATION, SMURFING, HAWALA, FINCEN, FATF, SAR, CTR, KYC, PEP, EDD, SANCTIONS, SCREENING, COMPLIANCE, SHELL, MULE, BEARER, OFFSHORE, and more.

### How to Play

**Selecting a cell:**
- Click any white cell to select it. The selected cell turns purple.
- The entire word containing that cell is highlighted in light purple.
- The clue for the active word appears in a banner above the grid.

**Setting the direction:**
- The puzzle has Across (left to right) and Down (top to bottom) words.
- If the selected cell belongs to both an Across and a Down word, click the same cell a second time to toggle between them.
- You can also press **Tab** on your keyboard to toggle direction.
- Arrow keys move the cursor and automatically set the direction.

**Typing letters:**
- Simply type a letter on your keyboard and it fills the selected cell. The cursor automatically moves to the next cell in the active direction.
- Letters you type appear in **white**.

**Erasing:**
- Press **Backspace** to erase the current cell and move back one cell.
- Press **Delete** to erase the current cell without moving.

**Using the clue panels:**
- The **Across** and **Down** clue lists appear to the right of the grid.
- Click any clue to jump directly to the first cell of that word.
- Completed words are automatically marked with a ✓ and shown in green.

### The Buttons

| Button | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Check** | Checks all your filled cells. Correct letters turn green, wrong ones turn red. |
| **Letter** | Reveals the correct letter in the currently selected cell (shown in amber). |
| **Word** | Reveals all letters in the currently active word (all shown in amber). |
| **Reset** | Clears everything and restarts the timer. |

### Timer & Progress
- The **timer** starts automatically and counts up from 0:00. Try to beat your previous time.
- The **progress percentage** shows what fraction of cells you have correctly filled, updating in real time after you press Check.

### Completion
When you fill in every cell correctly, a green **"Puzzle Complete!"** banner appears at the top showing your finishing time and how many words you placed.

### Tips
- Start with the short 3-letter words (SAR, KYC, PEP, CTR, EDD) — they are the easiest and help anchor the grid for longer words.
- Use **Reveal Letter** sparingly, only when you are truly stuck, to avoid spoiling the challenge.
- The clue panel highlights the active word's clue automatically — keep an eye on it as you navigate the grid.

## Study Plan Recommendation

Here is a suggested study order to get the most out of all five modules:

### Week 1–2 — Foundation
- Watch the **Animated Story** for context and motivation
- Read Chapter 1 of your CAMS Study Guide
- Work through the **Chapter 1 Flashcards** (20 cards)
- Answer all **Bank Secrecy Act** and **Money Laundering Typologies** practice questions

### Week 3–4 — Build Knowledge
- Complete flashcards for Chapters 2–5
- Work through all 200 practice questions, filtering by category
- Take **Mock Test 1** and **Mock Test 2** — note which areas score below 75%

### Week 5–6 — Consolidate
- Complete flashcards for Chapters 6–10
- Retake any mock test where you scored below 75%
- Switch flashcards to "Still Learning" mode and drill weak cards daily
- Play the **Crossword** to cement terminology — aim to finish without using Reveal

### Week 7 (Final Week)
- Take all five mock tests back-to-back in a single sitting to simulate exam endurance
- Focus practice questions on your weakest categories
- Do one final pass through all 200 flashcards

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does my progress save if I close the browser?**  
No. Progress is session-only. If you close the tab or browser, your quiz scores, flashcard marks, and crossword progress will reset. A future version may add save functionality.

**Can I use this on my phone?**  
The app works on mobile browsers, but it is designed primarily for desktop use. The crossword in particular is easier to use with a physical keyboard. On mobile, tapping a cell will bring up your device's virtual keyboard for letter input.

**Are the questions based on the official CAMS exam?**  
All content is aligned with the ACAMS CAMS Study Guide curriculum. The questions and flashcards mirror the topics, terminology, and concepts tested on the actual CAMS exam.

**What score should I aim for on the mock tests?**  
The official CAMS exam passing score is approximately 75%. Aim for 38 or more out of 50 (76%) on each mock test before you schedule your exam.

**Can I retake the mock tests?**  
Yes. Return to the test selection screen at any time and pick any of the five tests. There is no limit on retakes.

**What if I get stuck on the crossword?**  
Use the **Letter** button to reveal one cell at a time. If you are completely stuck on a word, use the **Word** button to reveal the entire word. Revealed letters show in amber so you know which ones you figured out yourself.
